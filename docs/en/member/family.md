# Family Information

## Goal

Review names, contacts, address, and individual/family photos.

## Before you begin

- Complete [Member sign-in](login.md).

## Steps

1. Check the family name and address at the top.
2. Scroll through family members and individual photos.
3. Select a photo to enlarge it; use close to return.
4. Choose **정보 수정** for a member who needs an update.

## Successful result

Only your family appears, and each edit control belongs to the correct person.

## Common problems

- **Missing/wrong family:** do not edit anything; sign out immediately and report it.
- **Photo missing:** the admin photo-display setting or network may be responsible.
