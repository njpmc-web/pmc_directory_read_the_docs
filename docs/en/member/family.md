# Family Information

## Goal

Review the household summary, each member’s contact information, and photos in the order shown.

## Before you begin

- Complete [Member sign-in](login.md).
- On a shared device, use **로그아웃** (sign out) when you leave the page.

## Steps

1. Confirm the household name in the page header.
2. In **가정 정보** (household information), review **교적번호** (household directory number), the member count, and the family photo. The camera button on the family photo begins a photo-change request.

<figure class="device-shot">
  <img src="../../../assets/images/review-framed/family-summary.png" alt="Family summary showing the household name directory number member count and family photo">
  <figcaption>First confirm the household name, <strong>교적번호</strong>, member count, and family photo.</figcaption>
</figure>

3. Scroll through the member cards. Each begins with the member’s name, church role, family relationship, age, and portrait.
4. Then review **영문 이름** (English name), **휴대폰** (phone), **이메일** (email), **주소** (address), and **교적번호** in that order. An unavailable value may appear as `-`.
5. Editable rows have a **수정** (edit) button. The camera button on a portrait begins a photo request for that member.

<figure class="device-shot">
  <img src="../../../assets/images/review-framed/family-members.png" alt="Family member card showing name relationship age portrait contact address and edit controls">
  <figcaption>Review each member’s photo and contact details, then choose <strong>수정</strong> beside a field that needs an update.</figcaption>
</figure>

## Successful result

Your household summary and each family member’s information appear for the correct person and in the expected order.

## Next step

Continue to [Edits and verification](edit.md) for a phone change or [Photo requests](photos.md) for a family photo. If another household appears or information is missing, do not edit it; see [Troubleshooting](../troubleshooting.md).
