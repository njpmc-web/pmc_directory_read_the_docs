# Photo Requests

## Goal

Select a family photo, fit it to the 4:3 frame, and submit it with an optional note.

## Before you begin

- Be signed in on [Family information](family.md).
- Prepare a JPG, PNG, WebP, HEIC, or HEIF family photo and obtain consent from the family members shown.

## Steps

1. In **가정 정보** (household information), choose the camera button labeled **가족 사진 변경 요청** on the family photo.

<figure class="device-shot">
  <img src="../../../assets/images/review-framed/photo-family.png" alt="Household information card with the family photo and photo-request camera button">
  <figcaption>Choose the camera button on the family photo to begin.</figcaption>
</figure>

2. In **사진 변경 요청** (photo change request), choose **사진 선택** and select a photo from the device.
3. In **사진 맞추기** (fit photo), drag the image and use **축소** (zoom out), **확대** (zoom in), rotate left, rotate right, or reset to fit the 4:3 area. Choose **완료** (done).

<figure class="device-shot">
  <img src="../../../assets/images/review-framed/photo-family-crop.png" alt="Family photo 4 by 3 crop area with zoom rotate reset and done controls">
  <figcaption>Adjust the 4:3 frame in <strong>사진 맞추기</strong>, then choose <strong>완료</strong>.</figcaption>
</figure>

4. Review the selected-photo preview. Choose **Crop** to refit it or **사진 변경** to select another photo.
5. If needed, enter a message for staff in **요청 메모 (선택)** (optional request note), then choose **요청 보내기** (send request).

<figure class="device-shot">
  <img src="../../../assets/images/review-framed/photo-family-notes.png" alt="Selected family photo preview with Crop change-photo optional note and send-request controls">
  <figcaption>Check the preview and optional note, then choose <strong>요청 보내기</strong>.</figcaption>
</figure>

6. Confirm **가족 사진 변경 요청이 접수되었습니다.** (family photo change request received) and the `Requested` label on the photo.

<figure class="device-shot">
  <img src="../../../assets/images/review-framed/photo-family-success.png" alt="Family-photo request received message and family preview labeled Requested">
  <figcaption>Confirm the received message and the <strong>Requested</strong> label.</figcaption>
</figure>

Member photos begin from the camera button on that member’s portrait and use the same select-and-fit pattern. Their crop area is labeled `프로필 사진 · 3:4`.

## Successful result

The family-photo request is received and the new preview is labeled `Requested`. The directory photo changes after staff process the request.

## Next step

Use [Manage requests](requests.md) to check the **가족 사진** request number and status. For file selection, upload, cancellation, or replacement problems, see [Troubleshooting](../troubleshooting.md).
