## Five steps for members

1. Enter the phone, choose **인증번호 받기**, and sign in with the six SMS digits.
2. Review the family summary and each member’s contacts and photos.
3. Choose **수정** for a field; for a phone change, finish with **지금 인증**.
4. Use the camera button on the family photo, select a photo, complete **사진 맞추기**, and send the request.
5. Under **요청 내역**, check `Request #`, responsible role, `Verified`, and processing status.

Details: [Member sign-in](member/login.md) · [Family information](member/family.md) · [Edits and verification](member/edit.md) · [Photo requests](member/photos.md) · [Manage requests](member/requests.md)

## Staff

1. At `/admin/login`, sign in with an approved Google account.
2. Open a menu allowed for your role.
3. Compare the request with directory records before processing it.
