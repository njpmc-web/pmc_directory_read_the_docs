# Quick Start

## Members

1. Choose **SMS 로그인** (SMS sign-in) or **이메일로 로그인** (sign in with email).
2. Enter the six-digit code sent to your registered contact.
3. Review family members and photos under **우리 가족 정보**.
4. Choose **정보 수정** on the item that needs changing.
5. Track progress under **요청 내역**.

## Staff

1. At `/admin/login`, sign in with an approved Google account.
2. Open a menu allowed for your role.
3. Compare the request with directory records before processing it.
