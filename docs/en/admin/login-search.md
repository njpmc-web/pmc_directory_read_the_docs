# Admin Sign-in and Member Search

## Goal

Sign in with an approved Google admin account and find a family by name or phone.

## Before you begin

- An approved Super Admin or Pastor Google account is required.

## Steps

1. Choose **Google로 계속** and use the approved account.
2. In **Member Search**, choose **이름** (name) or **전화번호** (phone).
3. Enter a query; compare city and phone ending among candidates.
4. Select the correct candidate and review family information and requests.

## Successful result

The selected family and related requests appear on the right.

## Common problems

- **Access denied:** use the approved account, not a personal Google account.
- **Same names:** compare city, family, and phone ending.
- **Menu missing:** Lookup Admin does not receive Member Search.
